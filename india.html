Taj Mahal

📍 Agra, Uttar Pradesh

One of the seven wonders of the world, the Taj Mahal is a symbol of eternal love built by Emperor Shah Jahan.

Amber Fort

📍 Jaipur, Rajasthan

Built from red sandstone and marble, this magnificent fort overlooks Maota Lake and features stunning architecture.

Ganges Ghats

📍 Varanasi, Uttar Pradesh

Experience the spiritual heart of India at the sacred ghats along the Ganges River with the famous evening Aarti ceremony.

Alleppey Backwaters

📍 Alleppey, Kerala

Cruise through the serene backwaters of Kerala on a traditional houseboat and experience the beauty of God's Own Country.

This UNESCO World Heritage Site is known for its spectacular Mughal architecture and beautiful gardens.

Munnar Tea Gardens

📍 Munnar, Kerala

Explore the picturesque tea plantations nestled among the rolling hills of this beautiful hill station.
